Schedule a Visit:

1)
Look at the list of outstanding visit request and pick select a visit that is close to you and fits your schedule.
2)
Review the visit information and plan the visit.
3)
Set a time that you will show up and a kind note with any additional information you want to send the visit requester.
4)
That's it! The visit will show up in your list of scheduled visits.

Your Visit:

1)
You will receive a reminder notification prior to your visit, so you don't forget, as will the requester so they will know you are coming.
2)
Show up with a smile on your face. If this is your first time meeting each other, make introductions and confirm you are here to fulfill their Visitry visit. Don't forget to have fun.

Post-Visit:

1)
After your visit you will be asked to provide feedback on how it went.
2)
Provide information about the person you visited (their mood, health, or anything interesting you learned) and how the visit went. These are good areas to include any non-urgent information that you want to communicate to the agency.
3)
Let us know how long you spent visiting and submit the feedback.
4)
If you enjoyed your experience go ahead and schedule another visit!

Tell Us What You Think:

1)
In the user actions menu select 'App Feedback'.
2)
Tell us about your experience using Visitry. Let us know if you had any problems using it, or if you have any ideas of how we can improve the application.
