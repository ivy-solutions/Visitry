Before you start make sure that you have download the app to your mobile device.


Roles:

There are three main roles that you can fit into. For more information about the roles click their link on the left.

Requester

This person is responsible for extending visit invitations. They pick the time and place of the visit.

Visitor

This person visits the requester. The primary job is to provide a wonderful time for the requester and have fun themselves.

Administrator

This person is in charge of running the friendly visiting program. They monitor the visits going on through their portal and make sure everyone is enjoying themselves

Creating an Account:

1)
When you start the app for the first time select 'New Account' and fill in all the information. When picking a role, see the descriptions above for help. (see Administrator for more details on registering as an admin.)
2)
You will then be asked to pick a group. This allows you to select which agency or organization you will be providing/receiving visits through. Your request to participate in the visiting program will be forwarded to the program administrator for approval.
3)
On your profile page you may provide a photo, update your location to the area where you will most likely be hosting visits or visiting, and provide any other information you would like to share. You will be able to come back to edit this profile at any time.
4)
You will receive an email asking you to verify your email address. If you are a visitor you may be contacted by the agency with additional information about their vetting process. Once the group administrator approves your membership you will be able to begin scheduling your visits.
5)
Enjoy your visits!
