Before you start make sure that you have download the app to your mobile device.


Roles:

There are three main roles that you can fit into. For more information about the roles click their link on the left.

Requester

This person is responsible for creating visits. They pick the time and place of the visit.

Visitor

This person visits the requester. The primary job is to provide a wonderful time for the requester and have fun themselves.

Administrator

This person is in charge of running the friendly visiting program. They monitor the visits going on through their portal and make sure everyone is enjoying themselves
